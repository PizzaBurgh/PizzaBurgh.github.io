Astrophotography - What is it?

This is a hobby I picked up in late 2015.

There are few levels to astrophotography

  • DSLR + Tripod
  • DSLR + Tracking Mount
  • DSLR or CCD + Telescope

I am currently working with a DSLR and a tracking mount

The tracking mount allows users to cancel out the rotation of Earth which allows for longer exposures and more details/stars
